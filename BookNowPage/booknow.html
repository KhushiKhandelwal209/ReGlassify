Benefits of Recycled Glass

Glass is 100% recyclable and can be recycled indefinitely without losing quality.Crushed glass is an environmentally friendly alternative to traditional aggregates like sand and gravel. It can be used as a fine aggregate in concrete, and it can improve the concrete's strength and durability.

Glass made from recycled glass reduces air pollution by 20% and water pollution by 50% compared to making new glass. Recycling glass saves raw materials like sand, soda ash, limestone, and feldspar. Making products from recycled glass uses 40% less energy than making new glass. This reduces emissions of greenhouse gases like carbon dioxide and nitrogen oxide.
